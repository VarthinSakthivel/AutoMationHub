Our Visits

Open Day at IISc Bengaluru on 2024

The visit to the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, during its Open Day was an eye-opening experience for our members. IISc, known for its world-class research and technological advancements, offered students a chance to witness groundbreaking projects across various engineering and scientific disciplines.


Members interacted with leading researchers, explored advanced laboratories, and gained insights into cutting-edge innovations in robotics, AI, and automation.


The Open Day provided a platform to understand the real-world applications of theoretical knowledge and inspired students to pursue research-driven careers while fostering a deeper passion for innovation and problem-solving.

Renuka High School, Bangalore

Our visit to Renuka High School, Bangalore, was a fulfilling outreach initiative aimed at empowering the next generation of engineers and innovators.


The club conducted an engaging seminar focused on career guidance, offering students insights into various engineering pathways, emerging fields in automation, and opportunities at Amrita University. The session included interactive discussions on life at Amrita, showcasing inspiring student projects, and demonstrating simple automation concepts.


Through these efforts, the club aimed to ignite curiosity, build confidence, and motivate students to consider careers in engineering, robotics, and technology-driven fields.

Honeywell - Industrial Visit

The industrial visit to Honeywell provided our members with an immersive experience into the world of industrial automation and smart technologies. Honeywell, a global leader in automation and control solutions, offered students a firsthand look at state-of-the-art systems used in manufacturing, building management, and safety operations.


Members observed live demonstrations of advanced control systems, IoT-integrated technologies, and data analytics tools. Experts from Honeywell guided students through practical applications of automation technologies in industries, emphasizing their role in improving operational efficiency, safety, and sustainability.


This visit provided valuable insights into the professional world, equipping students with practical knowledge and an understanding of industry standards and practices.


These visits play a crucial role in bridging the gap between classroom learning and real-world applications, equipping our members with skills, exposure, and inspiration to innovate and lead in the field of automation.
