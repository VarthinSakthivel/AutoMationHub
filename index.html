JIDO

JIDO is a vibrant club dedicated to advancing automation, robotics, and AI.
We engage in hands-on projects, educational visits, and collaborations to foster learning and innovation.

Our Mission

At JIDO, our mission is to inspire students by providing them with the skills and knowledge necessary for the future of automation, robotics, and AI. We focus on bridging the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world applications hrough hands-on projects, workshops, and collaborations.

What We Do

We organize a variety of events, workshops, and educational visits throughout Bangalore, offering our members exposure to industry practices and emerging technologies. These experiences help students enhance their practical understanding and gain insights into the workings of automation and robotics in the real world.

Get Involved

Joining JIDO provides opportunities to work on exciting projects, network with like-minded individuals, and develop valuable skills for your future career. Whether you're passionate about AI, robotics, or automation, JIDO is the perfect platform to nurture your skills, collaborate, and innovate with peers and industry experts.
